About me

Hi! I'm Missy and I'm really awesome. I was born on December/22/2017, and i was adopted on march 2018.

Every morning I like to wake up at exactly 6 in the morning, to move around the room to make noise and sometimes to bark, to go out for a walk.

After the walk, I like to be a little lazy until I get hungry. After I eat, I'll need another walk, and after that a well-deserved play. I don't like to take a bath in the evening, but it seems that I'm obligated to do this...

When I have time, I like to go to the beauty salon to find out the latest gossip. Recently, without necessarily wanting to, I started to learn a little programming
